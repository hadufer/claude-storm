## Posting notes (founder-led sales)

- The founder is the product. Lead or close with one line about who you are and what you build, so people follow a person, not just a repo.
- One primary ask. The star is the main call to action; the freelance line is secondary and self-qualifying. People who DM have already qualified themselves.
- Manual beats generic. The post sorts the room. After that, reply to every comment and DM yourself, short and specific, relevance before flattery.
- Format for the feed. Put the hook in the first two lines, since that is all that shows before "see more". Keep the bullets. A link in the body or the first comment both work now.
- Restart the algorithm. After 10 to 20 stars, post a short follow-up ("X stars in N days, thank you") to get a second wave.
